Paris

巴黎 · 四天
📍 Paris, France · 法国巴黎
Four days in the city of light — coffee in the morning, the river at dusk,
and the Eiffel Tower sparkling just for us at night.
在光之城的四天 —— 清晨的咖啡，黄昏的塞纳河，
夜里，埃菲尔铁塔为我们闪烁。

🗼 The icons 必看之地

Eiffel Tower 埃菲尔铁塔
The heart of Paris. Go up at sunset, or watch it sparkle on the hour after dark from the Champ de Mars lawn.
The Louvre 卢浮宫
The world's greatest museum. Quieter on Wednesday and Friday evenings.
Notre-Dame & Sainte-Chapelle 圣母院与圣礼拜堂
Gothic Paris on the Île de la Cité — Sainte-Chapelle's stained glass glows like jewels.
Arc de Triomphe & Champs-Élysées 凯旋门与香榭丽舍
Climb the arch for one of the best views down the grand avenue.

💙 Just for us 只属于我们

Seine river cruise at sunset 日落塞纳河游船
Glide past Notre-Dame and the lit bridges with a glass of champagne as the sky turns gold.
Sunrise at Trocadéro 特罗卡德罗的日出
Early morning, the Eiffel Tower view feels like it belongs only to the two of us.
Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur 蒙马特与圣心大教堂
Wander the hilltop village hand in hand, then watch the whole city from the basilica steps.
Jardin des Tuileries 杜乐丽花园
Fountains, flowers, and a quiet bench — slow Paris at its most beautiful.

🎨 If we want more 想再多一点

Musée d'Orsay 奥赛博物馆
The Impressionists — Monet, Renoir, Van Gogh — in a beautiful old train station.
Musée de l'Orangerie 橘园美术馆
Sit surrounded by Monet's giant Water Lilies. Calm and dreamlike.
Le Marais 玛莱区
Charming old streets, cafés, and little shops — perfect for an afternoon stroll.

✨ Tips to make the most of four days 四天小贴士

🎟️
Book the big sights now. Even in September, time slots for the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre fill up weeks in advance. 提前订票：即使在九月，埃菲尔铁塔和卢浮宫的时段也会提前几周订满。
🧥
Pack layers. Evenings cool to around 55°F (13°C), so bring a light jacket or sweater for outdoor dining. 备好外套：傍晚降到约13°C，户外用餐时带件轻外套或毛衣。
🍽️
Reserve dinner ahead. The romantic restaurants — especially anything in the Eiffel Tower — book out far in advance. 提前订餐厅：浪漫的餐厅，尤其是铁塔里的，要很早预订。
🚶
Go early, rest midday, glow at night. Mornings are crowd-free, and Paris is at its most magical after dark. 早出晚归：清晨人少，夜晚的巴黎最迷人。

🌤️ September weather 九月天气

Early-autumn Paris is gentle and golden — warm afternoons, cool evenings, thinner crowds than summer. A few rainy days are possible, so a small umbrella is worth tucking in the bag.
初秋的巴黎温柔而金黄 —— 午后温暖，夜晚微凉，人比夏天少。可能有几天下雨，包里带把小伞就好。

☀️ Day ~72°F / 22°C 🌙 Evening ~55°F / 13°C 🌂 A few rain showers 😊 Fewer crowds
