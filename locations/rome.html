🏛️ The icons 必看之地

The Colosseum 斗兽场
The mighty heart of ancient Rome. Standing inside this 2,000-year-old arena together is unforgettable — and in November the crowds are thin.
Roman Forum & Palatine Hill 古罗马广场与帕拉蒂尼山
Walk the ruins where emperors once lived, right beside the Colosseum.
The Vatican 梵蒂冈
St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums — climb the dome for the whole city at your feet.
The Pantheon 万神殿
A perfect ancient temple with light pouring through the open eye of its dome.

💙 Just for us 只属于我们

✨ How to prepare 出行准备

🧥
Pack layers. Bring a mix of long-sleeve shirts, sweaters, and a medium-weight jacket for cool mornings and evenings. 分层穿搭：长袖衫、毛衣，再加一件中等厚度的外套，应对早晚的凉意。
🌂
Bring rain gear. November is Rome's rainiest month — pack a reliable umbrella or waterproof parka, and comfortable water-resistant walking shoes. 带好雨具：十一月是罗马最多雨的月份 —— 带把好伞或防水外套，穿舒适防水的步行鞋。
🎟️
Book the major sites. Even in low season, reserve ahead for the Vatican Museums, the Colosseum, and special experiences like the Trevi Fountain visit to lock in your times. 提前订票：即使是淡季，梵蒂冈博物馆、斗兽场和许愿池等热门体验也要提前预订，锁定时间。

🌤️ November weather 十一月天气

Rome in November is mild and atmospheric — golden afternoons early in the month, cooler and a little rainy later on, but far fewer tourists, so the great sights feel almost like our own.
十一月的罗马温和而有氛围 —— 月初午后金黄，月末转凉略有雨，但游客少很多，宏伟的景点几乎像是我们专属的。

☀️ Early Nov ~68°F / 20°C 🌙 Late Nov ~52°F / 11°C 🌂 Rainiest month 😊 Quiet, few crowds
